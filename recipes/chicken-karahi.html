Chicken Karahi

Tomato-rich and restaurant-style, with the deep colour and heat that only freshly ground red chili powder can give — no shortcuts, no pre-mixed masala packets.

⌛ Prep 10 min 🔥 Cook 35 min 🍽 Serves 4

Ingredients

  • Chicken, cut into pieces 1/2 kg
  • Zaiqa red chili powder 1.5 tbsp
  • Tomatoes, chopped 4 large
  • Ginger-garlic paste 1 tbsp
  • Cooking oil or ghee 1/3 cup
  • Green chilies, slit 2
  • Cumin seeds 1 tsp
  • Coriander & julienned ginger garnish
  • Salt to taste

Sear the chicken

Heat oil in a karahi or heavy-bottomed pan. Add cumin seeds, then the chicken, and cook on high heat until it changes colour on all sides.

Build the base

Add ginger-garlic paste and cook for a minute until fragrant. Add the chopped tomatoes and cook, stirring occasionally, until they break down completely into a thick pulp.

Add the chili and cook it down

Stir in Zaiqa red chili powder and salt. Cook uncovered on medium-high heat, stirring often, until the oil visibly separates and rises to the top — this is what gives karahi its signature look.

Finish and serve

Add the slit green chilies and cook for another 5 minutes. Garnish with julienned ginger and fresh coriander. Serve hot with naan or roti.
