Chana Masala

A weeknight staple — chickpeas simmered with our organic coriander and red chili powder, finished with a squeeze of lemon. Ready in well under an hour.

⌛ Prep 10 min 🔥 Cook 25 min 🍽 Serves 4

Ingredients

  • Boiled chickpeas (chana) 2 cups
  • Zaiqa coriander powder 1.5 tbsp
  • Zaiqa red chili powder 1 tsp
  • Zaiqa turmeric powder 1/2 tsp
  • Onion, finely chopped 1 large
  • Tomatoes, chopped 2
  • Ginger-garlic paste 1 tbsp
  • Cooking oil 3 tbsp
  • Cumin seeds 1 tsp
  • Lemon juice & coriander leaves to finish
  • Salt to taste

Fry the base

Heat oil in a pot, add cumin seeds, then the chopped onion. Fry until golden brown.

Add the aromatics

Stir in ginger-garlic paste and cook for a minute, then add chopped tomatoes and cook until soft and pulpy.

Spice it

Add Zaiqa coriander powder, red chili powder, turmeric powder, and salt. Cook, stirring, until the oil separates from the masala.

Simmer with chickpeas

Add the boiled chickpeas along with a little water. Lightly mash a few chickpeas to thicken the gravy, then simmer for 10 minutes.

Finish and serve

Squeeze in fresh lemon juice, garnish with coriander leaves, and serve hot with steamed rice or naan.
