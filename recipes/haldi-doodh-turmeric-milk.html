Haldi Doodh (Turmeric Milk)

The warm, golden nightcap your ammi always made — just three ingredients, and the colour only comes through when the turmeric is actually fresh.

⌛ Prep 1 min 🔥 Cook 4 min 🍽 Serves 1

Ingredients

  • Milk 1 cup
  • Zaiqa turmeric powder 1/2 tsp
  • Honey or sugar 1 tsp
  • Black pepper pinch, optional

Warm the milk

Heat milk in a small saucepan over medium-low heat until warm — you don't want it boiling, just steaming gently.

Whisk in the turmeric

Add Zaiqa turmeric powder and a pinch of black pepper (it helps your body absorb the turmeric better). Whisk until fully dissolved — the milk should turn a deep golden colour.

Sweeten and serve

Remove from heat, stir in honey or sugar to taste, and sip warm — best enjoyed right before bed.
