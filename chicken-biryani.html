Chicken Biryani

Layered, fragrant, and built entirely around one spoon of our signature biryani masala — no ten-ingredient spice hunt, just the blend we already grind fresh for you.

⌛ Prep 20 min 🔥 Cook 40 min 🍽 Serves 4

Ingredients

  • Chicken, bone-in 1/2 kg
  • Zaiqa biryani masala 2 tbsp
  • Basmati rice, soaked 2 cups
  • Onions, sliced & fried 2 medium
  • Plain yogurt 1 cup
  • Tomatoes, chopped 2
  • Ginger-garlic paste 1 tbsp
  • Cooking oil 1/4 cup
  • Coriander & mint leaves handful
  • Salt to taste
  • Saffron or yellow colour optional

Marinate the chicken

Mix chicken with yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, half the fried onions and 1.5 tbsp Zaiqa biryani masala. Let it sit for at least 30 minutes — longer if you have time.

Cook the gravy

Heat oil in a heavy-bottomed pot, add chopped tomatoes and cook until soft and pulpy. Add the marinated chicken and cook on medium heat until tender and the oil separates at the edges.

Par-boil the rice

While the chicken cooks, boil the soaked rice in well-salted water until it's about 70% done — still firm at the centre. Drain immediately.

Layer it up

Spread the par-boiled rice evenly over the chicken gravy. Scatter the remaining fried onions, coriander, mint, and saffron water (or colour) on top.

Dum — the final steam

Cover tightly with a lid (seal the edges with dough or foil if it's loose) and cook on the lowest heat for 15-20 minutes. Let it rest for 5 minutes before opening and gently folding the layers together.
